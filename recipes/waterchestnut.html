Bacon-Wrapped Water Chestnuts

Home

Description

These bacon-wrapped water chestnuts are simply delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ketchup
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pound bacon
  • 2 (8 ounce) cans water chestnuts, drained

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine ketchup, brown sugar, and Worcestershire sauce in a medium bowl.
  3. Cut bacon strips in half. Wrap one slice of bacon around each chestnut and secure with a toothpick. Arrange water chestnut wraps in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes. Remove water chestnut wraps from the oven and drain some grease. Pour ketchup sauce over wraps.
  5. Bake until bacon is crispy and sauce thickens, 30 to 35 more minutes.
